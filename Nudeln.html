My Pasta Recipe

Ingredients for 4 Servings

  • 300g flour
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 3 eggs

Steps

Step 1: Prepare Ingredients

Prepare all ingredients.

Step 2: Mix Flour and Salt

Mix the flour and salt, creating a small well in the center.

Step 3: Prepare Eggs and Bowl

Prepare the eggs and a bowl.

Step 4: Crack the Eggs

Crack the eggs and place them in the bowl.

Step 5: Whisk the Eggs

Whisk the eggs thoroughly.

Step 6: Add Water to Eggs

Pour the eggs into a measuring cup and add water until it reaches 2 dl.

Step 7: Combine Ingredients

Pour the egg mixture into the well in the flour and salt.

Step 8: Mix Ingredients

Place the mixture in a dough mixer and mix for about 10 minutes.

Step 9: Knead the Dough

Knead the dough until smooth. If it's too wet, add flour; if too dry, knead with wet hands.

Step 10: Let the Dough Rest

Place the dough in a warm bowl with a damp cloth over it and let it rest for 1 hour.

Step 11: Divide the Dough

Divide the dough into smaller pieces.

Step 12: Flatten the Dough

Press the dough flat with your hands.

Step 13: Set Up the Pasta Machine

Set up the pasta machine.

Step 14: Roll the Dough

Run the dough through the machine, starting at level 1 and progressing to level 6.

Step 15: Halve the Dough

If the dough gets too long, cut it in half.

Step 16: Use the Noodle Cutter

Attach the noodle cutter and run the dough through.

Step 17: Hang the Noodles

Hang the noodles individually to dry.

Step 18: Let the Noodles Dry

Allow the noodles to dry completely. Once hardened, cook them like regular pasta.

Hope you like it!
